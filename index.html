Hi, I'm Ben =>

I am a software engineer with a team focused mentality and project oriented mindset. For the last twenty years I've worked professionally as a musician, producer and recording engineer. My experience touring with indie bands, recording as a session musician and playing in broadway orchestras has instilled a clear communication style with a supportive attitude; I strive to create positive environments where creativity and productivity can flourish. I began my coding journey in early 2022 and when I discovered the Advent of Code I was completely hooked: there is nothing more fun than sitting down with a good coffee and solving some puzzles. I have always had a passion for problem solving and building: whether it is making guitar effects pedals, repairing vintage synthesizers or modding my espresso machines I am always seeking out technical challenges that need solving. In my professional life I strive to keep focus trained on an end product a team can be proud of and a client will be elated with. I am experienced in full stack web development, creating elegant and accessible code and mapping traditional music creation to modern technological paradigms. I am continually striving to augment the ways I interface with art, music and technology.
