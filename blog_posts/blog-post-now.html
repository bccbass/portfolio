What am I up to now?

by Benjamin Campbell

June 28, 2023

Ted at the Grand Canyon Arizona, USA

Earlier this year I moved from Brooklyn, NY to Sydney... but not before driving across the US with my partner and my cat Ted. Highlights included visits to the Painted Desert, Petrified Forest and the Grand Canyon. After spending time with family in San Diego it was off to Sydney. I have completed my second term at the Coder Academy full-stack development program and am excited by the immense learning taking place; I've been especially enjoying the computer science and discrete math portions. I'm loving life in Australia and try to always get to Bunnings for a sausage sanga on the weekends.
