What am I up to now?

I've just moved to Sydney, Australia from Brooklyn, NY, with a brief 5 month stay in San Diego in between relocation while Ted(the cat)'s quarantine stay got squared away. In december I drove across the entire US with my partner and Ted, enjoying taking in the entire country as we went. Highlights included seeing family and visiting, Painted Desert/Petrified Forest National Park, and the Grand Canyon. I have completed my second week at Coder Academy full-stack bootcamp and am loving the immense learning taking place. I am getting settled in a new apartment, new country and a new career direction. I'm currently ideating on a few projects... PatchBey: a program to store patch settings/sounds for vintage analog synthesizer's lacking patch memory, PersonalModalities: a website to log and store different modalities used by people with rare diseases in order to find potential therapies not yet documented, and lastly, Who's on Bass?: For when you just want to know who's playing bass on a track.
